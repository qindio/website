Meet the team

Lorenzo Planas

Coffee & Code Amateur
  • @lorenzoplanas
  • lorenzoplanas.com

Thank you for stopping by. My name is Lorenzo, and I build business applications with Javascript, HTML5 and service-oriented APIs.

After a 10-year career as an IT governance and risk management consultant, in 2009 I founded my own business, a company called Qindio. We specialize in application assessments, drawing from our audit experience, and product development with down-to-earth software engineering principles.

We like starting from the business side, understanding processes and information flows, and then we dive down to incrementally build lean applications, mostly in Ruby, Python and Javascript. And lots of tests, right from the start.

Lorenzo likes sipping good Colombian coffee and dancing at cumbia and reggaeton classics.True story.

Karla Jaramillo

Lights! Camera! Code in my life!
  • @kavajaga
  • karlajaramillo.com

A code lover committed to finding out easy-to-use solutions to help users save time and effort.

I changed my path to dedicate my career to build intuitive and scalable solutions based on web app technology. After 6 year of working experience in diverse businesses, from consulting through production & purchasing to marketing, I realized that this cross-cutting approach needs to be tailored with software development. Then, I had the opportunity to join the GIS community to learn about ESRI products, where I discovered the perfect combination, Geographic Information Systems and programming.

I am passionate about discovering the hidden value of data. In countless cases data is related to a location, which means that a creative map can deliver key information.

I am determined to look for new ways to gain enriching experiences that allow me to grow in my developer career. I will share experiences through my blog.
